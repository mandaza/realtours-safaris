About Real Tours Safaris

Creating unforgettable African safari experiences since 2008

Know us better

At Real Tours Safaris, we specialize in curating unforgettable African experiences that connect travellers with the breathtaking beauty, vibrant cultures, and unparalleled adventures of Zimbabwe and beyond. Based in the heart of Victoria Falls, we pride ourselves on offering personalized tours that showcase the best of this natural wonder and its surrounding attractions.

From adrenaline-fueled activities to serene cultural immersions, our carefully crafted itineraries cater to adventurers, families, and explorers seeking meaningful connections with Africa's wilderness and heritage.

We are more than a touring company; we are storytellers, creating lifelong memories and moments of awe. With a dedicated team of passionate guides and a commitment to sustainable tourism, Real Tours Safaris ensures that every journey not only delights our guests but also benefits the communities and environments we touch.

Our Conservation Efforts

At Real Tours Safaris, conservation is at the heart of everything we do. We recognize that the incredible wildlife and habitats we showcase are precious resources that need protection.

We contribute a portion of every booking to local conservation projects focused on protecting Zimbabwe's wildlife and natural habitats. Our current initiatives include anti-poaching efforts in Hwange National Park and community education programs about wildlife conservation.

Our guides are also trained to educate visitors about conservation challenges and successes in Zimbabwe, helping to create global ambassadors for African wildlife conservation.
